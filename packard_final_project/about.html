Indulge in the Congo Experience

The natural beauty of our luxury destination resort awaits you. Rest assured you will experience the Congo's most beautiful rainforest in all its lush and awe inspiring greatness. All this just outside our doorstep.

This welcoming environment sets a tone for an experience you will never forget, and will return for, along with the stunning natural backdrop the Congo Rainforests are famous for the world over.

Rain forests such as these are the richest, oldest, most prolific, and most complex systems on earth, with a delicate ecological balance of flora and fauna.
